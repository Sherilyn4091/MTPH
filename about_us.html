About Us

Who we are

At Musttry PH, we believe that great food doesn’t have to be expensive or complicated.


Sometimes, all it takes is a banana. Our mission is to inspire home cooks, students, moms, and


aspiring food entrepreneurs to rediscover the value of this simple fruit that grows


in nearly every corner of the Philippines.


We’re proud to celebrate Filipino creativity, resourcefulness, and love for food

Sherilyn Sanchez

Executive Group General Manager

I am a student and the General Manager of our Web Development project, where we created a digital
cookbook. We believe that cooking is more than just preparing meals — it’s an expression of love, creativity,
and culture. Through every recipe, we share a story, a memory, and a piece of who we are. Our passion for
cooking comes from the joy it brings to others and the connections it creates around the table. As a team,
we poured our hearts into this project to celebrate the beauty of homemade dishes. This cookbook is our
way of inspiring others to cook with love and purpose.


Jannalyn Ycoy

Web Developer

I am a student and part of our Web Development project team. My role as a web developer allows me to
turn ideas into something real and interactive. Working on this digital cookbook, I discovered how
technology and creativity can come together to share something meaningful. Cooking may be an art,
but building a platform that brings recipes to life is just as rewarding. Every line of code we wrote
was inspired by our love for food and the stories behind each dish. As a student developer, I’m proud
to help create a space where people can connect, cook, and be inspired.
