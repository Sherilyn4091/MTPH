Maruya (Banana Fritters)
Recipe

Prep Time
10 minutes
Cook Time
10 minutes
Total Time
20 minutes
Cuisine
Filipino
Servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup flour
  • 0.5 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp melted butter
  • 1 cup oil
  • 6 bananas (saba), sliced
  • Sugar for sprinkling

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. 1 Mix  1  cup flour, 0.5  cup sugar,  1  tsp baking powder, and 0.25 tsp salt in a bowl.
  2. 2 Beat 1 egg with 1 cup milk and 2 tsp melted butter. Add to dry mixture.
  3. 3 Fold in 6 sliced bananas (saba).
