06
Banana Coffee Bread

Prep Time
25 minutes
Cook Time
50 minutes
Total Time
1 hour 15 minutes
Course
Dessert, Snacks/Merienda
Cuisine
American
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup ripe bananas (about 4 bananas), mashed
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur
  • 1 tablespoon instant espresso powder
  • 1 cup brown sugar (and extra for topping)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a 9x5-inch loaf pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on each side. Set aside.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients: Combine butter, bananas, sour cream, coffee liqueur, and espresso powder in a bowl and whisk until smooth. Add brown sugar and stir until combined. Add eggs one at a time, mixing thoroughly after each addition.
  3. Combine the dry ingredients: Sift flour, baking soda, and salt into the wet mixture. Fold the dry ingredients into the batter just until combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  4. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan, sprinkle with extra brown sugar, and bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    ⏰
    60:00
  5. Cool: Remove the banana bread from the pan using the parchment paper overhang. Transfer to a cooling rack and cool for 5 minutes before slicing with a serrated knife.
    00:05

Notes

Be careful not to overmix the batter. Overmixing can yield tough or chewy banana bread. Keep it soft and tender by folding the dry ingredients into the wet until just mixed.
