Cheesy Turon
Recipe

Prep Time
30 minutes
Cook Time
30 minutes
Total Time
60 minutes
Cuisine
Filipino
Servings: 10

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g Banana(sliced)
  • 0.5 g Cheese(sliced)
  • 20 Lumpia Wrapper (Spring Roll)
  • 4 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • Cooking Oil (to fry)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. 1 Place the sliced banana in the spring roll wrapper and add sliced cheese.
  2. 2 Preheat enough cooking oil in a pan, fry turon in batches.
    Add some sugar, wait until the sugar caramelizes and coats the turon. Cook for a few minutes
    until the turon turns golden brown.
  3. 3 Preheat enough cooking oil in a pan, fry turon in batches.
    Add some sugar, wait until the sugar caramelizes and coats the turon.
    Cook for a few minutes until the turon turns golden brown.

    00:00
  5. 4 Move cooked turon to a strainer to let excess oil drain. Serve and enjoy eating!
