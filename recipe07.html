07
3-Ingredient Pancakes

Prep Time
10 minutes
Cook Time
10 minutes
Total Time
20 minutes
Course
Snacks/Merienda
Cuisine
Filipino
Servings
2

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Mash banana in a bowl using a fork; add eggs, baking powder, and cinnamon and mix batter well.
  2. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Spoon batter into the hot butter and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
    03:00
  4. Enjoy!
