Minatamis na Saging Recipe (Banana with Caramel Sauce)

Prep Time
10 minutes
Cook Time
20 minutes
Total Time
30 minutes
Cuisine
Filipino
Servings: 4

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 pieces banana (saba variety), sliced diagonally
  • 1 Cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 0.25 1/4 Cup milk
  • crushed ice to serve

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. 1 In a pot, put the saging na saba, water and brown sugar.
  2. 2 Boil in low heat, allowing the saba to cook and the sugar
    to caramelized. Stir constantly on low flame until the caramel is thick.
  3. 3Place the saba mixture in a serving bowl,
    pour some milk and add crushed ice (optional).
  4. ⏰
    00:20
  5. 4Serve and enjoy eating!
